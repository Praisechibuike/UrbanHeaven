FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Send us a message using the form above.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery within Nigeria takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available at checkout for an additional fee. International orders may take 7-14 business days depending on your location.

What is your return and refund policy?

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy. Items must be in their original condition with tags attached. Simply contact us with your order number and reason for return, and we'll arrange a collection or drop-off point near you. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over N50,000 within Nigeria. Orders below N50,000 attract a flat shipping fee of N2000. We periodically run free shipping promotions for all orders — subscribe to our newsletter to be notified.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our courier partner's website to track your parcel in real time. If you experience any issues with tracking, contact us directly and we'll assist immediately.

Are your products authentic and good quality?

Absolutely. At UrbanHeaven, we source every product directly from verified suppliers and brands. We perform quality checks before dispatching orders. If you ever receive a product that doesn't meet our quality standards, we will replace it or issue a full refund — no questions asked.

Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 12 hours of being placed, as long as they have not yet been dispatched. Contact our support team as soon as possible via phone or email. Once an order has been collected by our courier, it cannot be cancelled but you can still initiate a return once it arrives.
